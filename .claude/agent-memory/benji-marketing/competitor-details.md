# Competitor Details

## GlassFrog
- Holacracy-specific (not sociocracy-friendly)
- No real-time chat, no Kanban
- Free tier: 10 users, basic features, unlimited circles/roles
- Premium: $7/user/month, includes Slack/Asana/JIRA integrations, SAML SSO
- Add-ons: Goals/OKRs $1.50/user, FrogBot AI $1.50/user
- 30-day free trial, no credit card
- URL: glassfrog.com/pricing

## Loomio
- Cooperative-owned, strong in co-op/nonprofit space
- Async decision-making only, no real-time chat
- Starter: $399/yr commercial ($299 nonprofit), 30 members max
- Pro: $999/yr commercial ($499 nonprofit), 300 members, $100/yr per extra 100
- Private Host: $8,999/yr (unlimited, dedicated server)
- 14-day trial, no free tier
- URL: loomio.com/pricing

## Holaspirit / Talkspirit
- Holaspirit merged into Talkspirit (301 redirect)
- Talkspirit: ~$5-13 EUR/user/month across Standard/Premium/Business tiers
- Combined governance + communication platform
- More enterprise-focused, complex onboarding
- French company

## Slack (adjacent)
- Free / $7.25 Pro / $12.50 Business+ / $15+ Enterprise per user/month
- 90-day message history on free tier
- No governance features whatsoever
- AI bundled into Business+ as of 2025

## Notion (adjacent)
- Free / $10 Plus / $20 Business per user/month
- AI bundled into Business tier as of May 2025
- General-purpose workspace, no governance tools
- No real-time chat
